c:solve

p:We can tidy this solution by typing <i>clean</i> or by pressing the <b>Clean</b> button.
h:toolbar-tidy

h:btn-refine;1

c:sel atoms where xatom.uiso < 0.035&&xatom.type!='Q'

h:btn-elementO

p:Refine this again...
c:refine

h:toolbar-anis
c:anis
c:refine

p:... clean it ...
c:clean

p:Let's add some hydrogen atoms to this. Type <i>hadd</i> or click on the <b>ADD H</b> button:
h:button_small-add_h
c:hadd
c:refine
